About Me

Hi, I'm Thato!

My story begins back when I was a kid. I had a burning desire to be creative and have a positive impact in the world's evolution and whilst most kids have some superhero complex desires when they are younger, they eventually outgrow it but NOT ME. Growing up in Botswana, I became fascinated by how the internet connects people and ideas across the world and that curiosity slowly evoked my creative passion for building digital experiences. Web development felt like the perfect blend of creativity and problem solving. It gave me a way to turn ideas into something real, something people could see, use, and enjoy.

My ambition as a developer is to build technology that solves real problems and helps people work smarter. Along with web development, I am working toward becoming a data analyst specialist who can guide organizations in using data for competitive advantage, efficiency, and customer satisfaction. By blending these skills, I hope to create digital experiences that are not only functional and creative but also driven by meaningful data insights.

Feel free to explore my portfolio to view my skills and projects I have accomplished in my learning path so far.
